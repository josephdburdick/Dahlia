I am a filmmaker/producer based in NYC. I work in all types of audiovisual productions including Narrative Films, Documentaries, Music Videos and Commercials.

Originally a business woman, I studied Business Administration in the American University of Paraguay and specialized in Business Management in the Ecole Supérieure de Commerce de Montpellier in France. I also have a diploma in Performing Arts and Filmmaking from the New York Film Academy.

I dedicated the first half of my career to project management and consulting in different fields such as Non-Profit, Advertising and Commercial. I got additional training through intensive workshops and business seminars in fields that would make me the best leader and communicator such as coaching, leadership, writing, speech, etiquette, and customer service.

I was introduced to filmmaking while shooting a commercial for a client. I fell in love with the production process and realized the power of this communication tool. Since then I have been through almost all departments including Directing, AD, Camera, Lighting, Grip, Sound and Production. Combining my solid technical, managerial and communication skills with my innate creativity I am now a unique asset for any audiovisual project.
